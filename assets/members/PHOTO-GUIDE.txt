To add a real photo:
1. Crop the portrait approximately 4:5 vertically.
2. Export it as PNG.
3. Give it the exact filename above.
4. Replace the placeholder file in assets/members/.

You do not need to edit about.html when the filename stays the same.

Recommended export:
- 1200 x 1500 px
- PNG or high-quality JPG converted to the required PNG filename
- Person centered with some room above the head
